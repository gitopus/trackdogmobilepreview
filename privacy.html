Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 16, 2025

1. Introduction and Scope

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Trackdog, Inc. ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, processes, and discloses personal information about users ("you" or "your") of our music analytics and discovery platform, including our website, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the "Service"). This Policy applies to all information collected through the Service and any related communications.

By accessing or using our Service, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Policy. If you do not agree with the practices described herein, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide: We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including but not limited to:

  • Account registration information (name, email address, username, password);
  • Profile information and preferences;
  • Communications with our customer support;
  • Survey responses and feedback;
  • Payment information (processed by third-party payment processors).

2.2 Automatically Collected Information: We automatically collect certain information when you use our Service:

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers);
  • Usage data (pages visited, features used, time spent, click-through rates);
  • Log data (access times, error logs, referral URLs);
  • Location information (general geographic location based on IP address).

2.3 Third-Party Platform Data: When you connect third-party music streaming accounts (such as Spotify), we collect:

  • Music listening history and statistics;
  • Playlists and saved music;
  • Account profile information;
  • Social connections and following relationships (if applicable).

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual Necessity: To perform our contractual obligations under our Terms of Service;

  • Consent: Where you have provided explicit consent for specific processing activities;
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

4.2 Service Improvement:

  • Developing new features and functionalities;
  • Conducting research and analytics;

4.3 Communications:

4.4 Legal and Security:
